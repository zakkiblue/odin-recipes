Cah Kangkung

Description

Cah kangkung is an Indonesian-Chinese dish of stir-fried water spinach. This extremely simple recipe requires just onion, garlic and oyster sauce for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of kangkung (water spinach)
  • 0.5 onion, peeled, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • A pinch of white pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 0.5 cube vegetable stock (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut the kangkung into bite-sized pieces, wash thoroughly, set aside.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a wok over medium high heat.
  3. Saute the onion and garlic for 2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the kangkung.
  5. Add the salt, pepper powder and vegetable stock. Mix well
  6. Add the oyster sauce, cook for another 1 minute.
  7. Turn off the heat and transfer to a serving plate.
